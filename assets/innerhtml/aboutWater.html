Tools used:
High Level Shader Language, GLSL, ShaderLab, Unity

Description:
The technique used in this tutorial was described in detail by Alex Vlachos from Valve, in the SIGGRAPH2010 presentation Water Flow in Portal 2.
What I thought would be a C# project evolved into Computer Shaders, pushing math jobs onto the GPU, using the profiler to track bottlenecks and learning how ocean waves do not conform to sin/cos waves.
