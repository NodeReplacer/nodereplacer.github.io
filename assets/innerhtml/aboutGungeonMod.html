Tools used:
C#, .NET framework, DNSpy, Disassemblers

Description:
This mod doubles chest drops and raises item drop chance.
The enemies have increased health in co-op but this means that it uses more ammo to defeat enemies.
These two players also have to share resources meant for one player between two people.
This means that it's likely that the players won't have enough guns or powerups in a game about having wacky guns modified by fun powerups.
Completely running against the central concept of the game.
